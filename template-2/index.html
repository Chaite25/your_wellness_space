Kogan 65” QLED 4K Tizen Smart TV

Delivering stunning 4K resolution and vibrant colors, the Kogan 65” QLED Tizen Smart TV combines performance with user-friendly features for an exceptional viewing experience. With voice assistant integration, quick-access buttons, and built-in Samsung features like Samsung TV Plus, this TV brings convenience and entertainment directly to your fingertips.

Key Features

  • Ultra HD 4K Resolution: Provides breathtaking picture clarity and detail.
  • QLED Technology: Quantum Dot technology creates vivid, lifelike color reproduction.
  • High Dynamic Range (HDR): Brighter highlights, enhanced contrast, and realistic colors for an immersive experience.
  • Easy-Access Buttons: Quick buttons for Netflix, Amazon Prime, and Disney+ on the remote.
  • Samsung TV Plus: Enjoy over 100 live streaming channels, including news, sports, and entertainment, for free with an internet connection.
  • Samsung App Store: Download all your favorite apps for endless entertainment.
  • SmartThings Integration: Control and monitor your smart home devices from the TV.
  • Voice Assistants: Compatible with Google Home and Bixby for hands-free control.
  • Comprehensive Connectivity: Wi-Fi, Ethernet, and HDMI support for easy media playback.

About Tizen OS

Tizen OS offers a streamlined, user-friendly interface that provides easy access to apps like Netflix, YouTube, and Amazon Prime. With SmartThings integration, control of your smart home devices is a breeze, while multiple connectivity options ensure seamless compatibility with external devices.

Samsung TV Plus

Access free streaming with Samsung TV Plus, offering over 100 channels, including news, sports, and entertainment, all without a subscription. Constantly updated, this feature ensures there’s always something fresh to watch.

Gaming Mode

Explore and play over 3000 games, from console titles to streaming cloud games, with no console required. Simply connect a controller and dive into games from Xbox Game Studios, NVIDIA GeForce Now, and more.

SmartThings Hub

With SmartThings, manage and monitor all your connected devices through an intuitive interface. Receive real-time notifications and control compatible devices directly from your TV screen.

Sleek Remote and Voice Control

The remote’s Bluetooth design and Bixby voice assistant make navigation easy. Search for shows, open apps, and control settings with simple voice commands or through Google Home for added convenience.

High-Definition HDR

With HDR support and 4K resolution, the Kogan 65” QLED TV brings out deeper contrasts and vibrant colors, creating a true-to-life viewing experience.

Freeview Access

Stream over 30 live and on-demand TV channels for free through Freeview, with no hidden fees or contracts required.
